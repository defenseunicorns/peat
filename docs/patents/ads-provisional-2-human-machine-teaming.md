## Prior Disclosure and Related Work

**Related Government-Sponsored Project**: Defense Innovation Unit (DIU) - COD (Collaborative Operations in Denied Environments) project (2021-2023)

**Inventor's Prior Employment**: Inventor Kit Plummer was employed at Ditto Technologies and contributed to the COD project during 2021-2023.

**Differentiation from Prior Work**:
This invention was developed independently at (r)evolve LLC (2024-2025) and is substantially different from COD work. Key novel features NOT in COD:
- Authority-weighted leadership election combining military rank and technical capability
- Cognitive load and fatigue monitoring with dynamic authority adjustment
- Role-based task assignment with MOS (Military Occupational Specialty) matching
- Tunable election policies (RankDominant, TechnicalDominant, Hybrid, Contextual)
- Hierarchical authority constraint propagation

**Government Rights**: This invention was NOT developed under government contract and NO government rights apply. Development funded entirely by (r)evolve LLC.

**Coordination with Government**: Inventors have proactively coordinated with DIU program manager to ensure transparency regarding relationship to prior COD work and confirm no government rights or obligations.
